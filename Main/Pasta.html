    If you’re a vegetarian, you’ll love Infinity's Veg Extravaganza, Indi Tandoori Paneer, and Mexican Green Wave. Though these are the most loved Infinity's pizzas along with the cheesy Margherita pizza, you should check out the Infinity's menu to see what you like the most.
