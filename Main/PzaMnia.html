FAQ - People Also Ask

  1. What is Pizza Mania?

    Pizza mania is the budget section of this brand wherein they offer several pizza options in veg and non-veg along with starters and side dishes in combinations and at reduced prices. The orders and sizes can depend on the person placing the order. One can also choose different fresh toppings.

  2. Which cheese is used in Pizza Mania?

    Domino’s ensures the use of the best-quality and freshest cheese options present in the market. Particularly for pizza mania, they use mozzarella cheese that provides the stringiness and chewiness people desire. Additionally, based on what the order needs, they customize and use a combination of different cheeses for the best taste.

  3. What are the offers available for Pizza Mania?

    There are several Domino’s coupons that can be availed not only for the special menu for pizza mania but also for other side dishes like chicken wings, desserts, etc. Everyday value offer for 2 medium pizzas, Dominos new users offers for first-time customers, and several more can be opted for.
